# Design Partner Offer

**Not sponsorship. Not a sale.** A design partnership: one painful workflow, two weeks, a case study.

## Who this is for
An AI-native or fast-scaling team whose company knowledge is fragmented across tools, where agents need to act on *trusted* facts — and where a reachable owner can pilot one workflow.

## The pilot
- **One workflow** (e.g. support-answer accuracy, incident/runbook recall, or "have we decided this before?").
- **2 weeks.** We ingest that workflow's existing knowledge into a scoped Brain, stand up grounded + abstaining retrieval, and wire the draft→quarantine→human-stamp loop for it.
- **Deliverable:** a before/after on a small eval set built from your real questions — hallucination/error reduction, plus "the owner would trust it."

## What we build vs what's out of scope (2 weeks)
- ✅ Scoped corpus, grounded+cited retrieval, abstention bar, human-gated writeback for the one workflow.
- ❌ Full connector suite, safe auto-write, executable-skill generation, SSO/security certification.

## What the partner provides
- One workflow owner (a few hours/week), scoped **read** access to that workflow's knowledge (no PII/secrets), ~15 real questions, and feedback.

## Security boundaries
- Runs **local / your cloud**; open markdown over git = auditable, exportable, deletable.
- Brain stores **policy/knowledge, never raw PII/secrets/payment data**.
- Ingestion is **human-reviewed** during the pilot (the auto-sanitize layer is in build).
- Clear exit: it's files — keep or delete them.

## The ask (non-cash)
- Your **feedback**, a **reference call**, and **case-study rights** (named or anonymized).
- Optional: a letter of intent ("would pay $X/mo if it hits Y").

## Success criteria (agreed up front)
Grounded + cited answers, **abstention on unknowns (no confident wrong answers)**, measurably fewer hallucinations than the current approach, and the workflow owner saying "I'd trust this."
